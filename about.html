ABOUT ME

I am Kudumula Lokeswar Reddy, currently working as an Assistant Professor at KSRM College of Engineering.
My journey reflects determination and perseverance with a deep commitment to education and personal growth.
I come from a humble farming family where agriculture has been our primary livelihood for generations.
Growing up in this environment taught me the importance of hard work, patience, and responsibility.
I completed my early education in a Government Telugu-medium school with limited resources and exposure.
During my journey, I worked in different roles including farming, driving, and delivery services to support my education.
These experiences strengthened my discipline, resilience, and work ethic.
Later, my interest developed toward technology, teaching, and knowledge sharing.
I developed knowledge in Database Management Systems, Data Warehousing, SQL, PL/SQL, and Full Stack Web Development.
Previously, I worked as an Assistant Professor at Annamacharya University.
I also served as NSS Incharge for the AIML Department and Placement Incharge for AIML and CSE(AI).
Currently at KSRM College of Engineering, I focus on helping students develop industry-ready skills and strong technical foundations.
I strongly believe that education can transform lives regardless of background.
My journey from agricultural fields to engineering education proves that determination can overcome any obstacle.
